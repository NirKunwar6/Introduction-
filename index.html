About Nirdeshan Kunwar

Born in Achham, Nepal, I began my educational journey at Shree Kinder Garden Academy in Sanfebagar. I now reside in Kathmandu, where I balance professional cricket with personal development and studies at GoldenGate International College.

I am deeply grateful to my parents, Devraj Kunwar and Kaushilla Devi Kunwar, and my brother Lokendra Kunwar, whose unwavering support has been instrumental in my journey. They remain my greatest source of motivation and guidance throughout my career.

As a professional cricketer, I am committed to representing Achham District with honor and pride. I train at the prestigious Kathmandu Cricket Academy, where I continue developing as an elite athlete. Every match is an opportunity to improve, and every tournament is a platform to demonstrate dedication to excellence.

I believe in leading by example through disciplined training, strategic play, and a championship mentality. My commitment to professional cricket is unwavering, and my goal is to achieve success at the highest levels of the sport and bring pride to my district and nation.
