About Me

Professional Cricket | Discipline | Excellence

Born in Achham, Nepal, I am a dedicated professional cricketer with proven excellence at the national level. My educational foundation was built at Shree Kinder Garden Academy in Sanfebagar before pursuing my cricket ambitions in Kathmandu.

I currently train at the prestigious Kathmandu Cricket Academy, where I continue to refine my skills as an elite athlete. My journey has been defined by discipline, consistency, and unwavering commitment to representing my district with pride.

Deeply grateful to my parents Devraj Kunwar and Kaushilla Devi Kunwar, and my brother Lokendra Kunwar, whose support has been instrumental in my success. I believe in leading by example through professional conduct and championship mentality.

About Nirdeshan Kunwar

Born in Achham, Nepal, I began my educational journey at Shree Kinder Garden Academy in Sanfebagar. I now reside in Kathmandu, where I balance professional cricket with personal development and studies.

I am deeply grateful to my parents, Devraj Kunwar and Kaushilla Devi Kunwar, and my brother Lokendra Kunwar, whose unwavering support has been instrumental in my journey. They remain my greatest source of motivation.

As a professional cricketer, I am committed to representing Achham District with honor. I train at the prestigious Kathmandu Cricket Academy, where I continue developing as an elite athlete. Every match is an opportunity to improve and demonstrate dedication to excellence.

I believe in leading by example through disciplined training, strategic play, and championship mentality. My goal is to achieve success at the highest levels of the sport and bring pride to my district and nation.
